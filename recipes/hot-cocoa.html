Creamy Hot Cocoa

Description

It's old fashioned, it's comforting, it makes the kitchen smell wonderful and it's good for the soul.

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • ⅓ cup boiling water
  • 3 ½ cups milk
  • ¾ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup half-and-half cream
